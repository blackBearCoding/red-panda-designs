Meet the Owner

Hey there, I’m Delaney!
Owner and Chief Creative at Red Panda Designs.

When determining what I wanted to do for a living, I had two criteria: love what I do and help people. My passion for art and design started with my youthful doodles as a child. Since I could pick up a pencil, I’ve been an artist—art has always been a creative outlet that drives my purpose!

So, after studying graphic design at Warren Technical School and completing a software development apprenticeship at Techtonic in Boulder, CO, I decided to take my art and tech skills and form a multidisciplinary design agency that helps small business owners bring their visions to life. Cue the beginning of Red Panda Designs!

Art and design are innate parts of my being, a gift that I love sharing with my clients. Each project gives new meaning to my mission and I am fueled by working with so many unique individuals and imaginations daily!

I believe that when dreaming up creative ideas, it’s so important to vibe with your designer! So, let’s get to know each other. Here are some fun facts about me:

• My family used to own the Telluride Valley Floor, so I grew up traipsing around some of the most incredible natural landscapes in the country. I think it fueled my love for beautiful things!

• I am ambidextrous, which is a fancy way of saying I can do anything with either hand. This comes in super handy for an artist and designer since my hands are my most prized tools of the trade!

• Creativity is always flowing through my veins. I love to perform on stage. Dancing, acting, singing—you name it, I love it. But I never said I was good at it… we’ll call it my passion project.

• I have a secret superpower: ADHD. Now I guess the secret’s out, but I want to end the stigma around mental illness! ADHD fuels my empathy, makes me a super passionate human, and inspires creative sparks others don’t experience. It’s just part of what makes me who I am! Now, I want to learn more about you!

Let’s chat about your upcoming web and graphic design projects and determine how we can elevate your brand and web design together.
